Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about our roofing and construction services? We've got answers.

Do you offer free quotes?

Yes! We provide free, no-obligation quotes for all roofing, construction, and renovation projects. Call us at +27 73 229 8399 or fill out our online form.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. Yonke Roofing (PTY) LTD is a fully registered and licensed construction company in the Eastern Cape, South Africa.

What are your operating hours?

We are open Monday to Friday from 9 AM to 5 PM. We're closed on Saturdays and Sundays. For urgent inquiries, call us during business hours.

Timeline depends on the scope. A standard roof replacement typically takes 3-7 days, while new construction varies based on size and complexity.

Yes! We specialize in both residential and commercial/industrial roofing and construction projects of all sizes.

Which areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Bethelsdorp, Port Elizabeth CBD, Newton Park, Walmer, Summerstrand, Greenacres, Despatch, and surrounding areas in the Eastern Cape.
